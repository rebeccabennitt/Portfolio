ABOUT ME

My name is Rebecca Bennitt. I'm a designer and developer.
I have a variety of interests, and I like being busy, have an incredible work ethic and am a bit of a jack of all trades. In my previous lives, I have worked in a variety of fields -- radio news, event planning/floral design, retail and restaurant management, and most recently PR & administrative work in higher education. However, I always managed to be asked to help design, build or maintain several of my employers' websites. I developed a real passion for user experience design and frontend web development.
